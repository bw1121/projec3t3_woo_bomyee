Bojack’s Career Timeline

Standup Comic

Performed comedy routines at hole-in-the-wall comedy clubs with his friend Herb, trying to become big stars.

Horsin’ Around

Hit TV comedy series starring BoJack. This put Bojack’s name on the map and he rose to fame.

Restaurant Elephante

Owner of a restaurant in Hollywoo.

The BoJack Horseman Show

Originally titled “Mitch’s Life” was critically panned and canceled.

One Trick Pony

Biography about the life of BoJack written by Dian Ngyuen.

Secretariat

Movie based on the life of Secretariat, a 1973 Triple Crown winner
